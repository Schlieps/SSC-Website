Friendship Circle of Washington

Friends are not a luxury. They’re a necessity.

Here at the Friendship Circle we bring together children with special needs and teen volunteers to create a bond that lasts a lifetime.  We empower children and teens with special needs, helping them develop the skills they need to live a productive and independent adult life. We empower our teen volunteers, helping them develop the confidence, self-esteem, and skills they need to become the leaders of tomorrow. We nurture community, working to ensure that inclusion and diversity are the norm, not the exception and that every individual has the opportunity to participate and succeed.
