Long Nguyen

Long has been in the Seattle area since he was 4 and has loved the area ever since. He is very passionate about helping people across various socioeconomic classes increase their technical literacy.

He is an alum of the University of Washington, with a focus in computer science. As a software engineer, he loves tackling new projects and solving real world problems with technology. He is also very passionate in promoting diversity in the computer science field and loves taking on volunteer opportunities to do so.

Outside of work, he enjoys travel around all over the world and being in the great outdoors. In the wintertime, you can find him either on the slopes of Stevens Pass, Crystal Mountain, or Whistler. In the summertime, you can find him hiking around the endless amount of trails Washington has to offer.
