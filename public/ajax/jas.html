Jas Banwait

President

Jas arrived from India when he was 3. He remained strongly connected with Punjabi culture through his family roots. Now he's devoted to bridging the intercultural gaps, by organizing events that boast high diversity in the Seattle community.

Jas is an alum of UW, who works at a boutique consulting firm as a Technical Product Manager.

Outside of work and managing Seattle Social Circle, Jas has backpacked through 20+ countries and has been a contestant on The Price Is Right.

He’s also a HUGE Marvel fan and spends hours reading, watching videos, and going to every of the movie premieres.

We already know he’s looking forward to putting out more kick-ass events for you all to enjoy!
