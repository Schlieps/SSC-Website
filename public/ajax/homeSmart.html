Chris' journey into a career as a real estate professional was via the U.S. Navy. Seriously! After graduating from the U.S. Naval Academy as a young naval officer, Chris befriended a retired Navy couple who were real estate brokers. They took him under their wing and explained that real estate was a career field ideally suited to him because it would play to his strong suits as a detail-oriented analytical thinker, consummate social butterfly, and most importantly, allow him to continue to serve friends and clients post-Navy.
