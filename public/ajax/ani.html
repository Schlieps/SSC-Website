Ani Nguyen

Creative Director

Ani moved from Vietnam to the U.S. when she was 8 years old with her family. She has strong ties to her culture and the history as her grandfather is a war veteran, and so are many other family members. She has been involved in and has great interest in human rights issues and charitable causes.

She is an alum University of Washington, with a concentration in Electrical Engineering. She devotes her engineering knowledge as a research engineer at UW Medicine Applied Physics Laboratory. There she helps develop non-invasive technology that treat kidney stones and tumors.

Ani has a passion for arts, in fact it was her love of art that ironically lead her to become an engineer.
