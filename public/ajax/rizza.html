Rizza Bagan

Managing Director

Rizza was born and raised in the Philippines. She moved to Washington State 7 years ago after she completed her degree of Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management. Travelling the world, studying cultures and learning people’s different beliefs in life has always been her love and interest, she is always curious with what the world offers as she believes that there is a lot more to life out there.

One of the hobbies that Rizza loves to do is hiking – she’s conquered even one of the biggest mountains in Washington State, including Mt. Adams, St. Helens, day through hiked The Enchantments & many more. She is now preparing and planning to do the Mt. Rainier summit. Rizza loves to share her culture by cooking. She loves to cook and have a small gathering with friends – sharing different dishes and enjoying each other’s company. In her country, hospitality and food is important whenever there are invited guests or friends. One of Filipino’s belief is that food’s what brings joy and makes everybody closer together.

Rizza has also experience with the runway modelling. She has walked with some well known international and local designers such as GG connections, Gustavo Apiti Couture, Lisa Marie Couture & many more. She is a published editorial model as well - but is now moving towards producing fashion shows & events, and have more focus with her Real Estate Career.

If you have any interest of hiking, cooking, modelling, don’t hesitate to reach out to Rizza! She is very passionate and willing to help.
